How to make honey muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, and vanilla.
  3. Add dry mix to liquid in two batches mixing thourouly.
  4. Put mix in greased muffin tins.
  5. Bake for 16min, or until lightly brown.
  6. Enjoy :)
